Chicken Adobo Recipe

Description

The Chicken Adobo is a Filipino dish. Adobo itself is diverse among Filipino because every household have a different version of it.

It is also an easy dish to cook as it was my first dish that I cooked myself

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups of water
  • half cup of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of white vinegar
  • 4 cloves of garlic(sliced)
  • 1 regular sized onion(sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon of crushed black pepper

Steps

  1. Put all of the ingredients into a pot
  2. Cover and let simmer for 45 minutes. Stir occasionally to avoid ingredients sticking in the bottom
  3. Prepare and serve
